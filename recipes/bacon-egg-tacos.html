Bacon and Egg Tacos

Description

This is a simple to prepare and cook recipe for delicious bacon and egg breakfast tacos. This is the perfect meal for peole who need something to eat on the go.

Most of the ingredients needed for this recipe can be found online.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Ground Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup of Crumbled Cooked Bacon
  • 3 Slices of American Cheese, Diced
  • 1 Cup of Salsa (optional)
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 6 Flour Tortillas
  • 6 Eggs

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs together in a bowl, stir bacon.
  2. Melt butter in a skiller over medium heat. Add egg mixture; cook and stir until eggs are completly set, 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in American cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Wrap tortillas in damp paper towel; microwave until warmed through, 30 second to a 1 minue.
  4. Spoon 1/4 cup egg mixture into the center of each torilla; fold sides to cover. Serve with salsa.
